How the Job Scout Agent Works

  • Autonomous Job Searching: The agent automatically fetches new jobs daily from external job boards
  • Smart Matching: Uses AI to analyze each job against your resume and skills
  • Quality Filtering: Only notifies you of matches above your threshold ({{ config.match_threshold }}%)
  • Learning from Feedback: The agent learns from your "Interested/Not Interested" feedback to improve future matches
